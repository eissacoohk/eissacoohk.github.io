Set in Motion

Branding and Wayfinding

ABOUT

As designers we are always creating, thinking and experimenting to make our ideas come to life. The Set in Motion Exhibition, showcased student projects of AUT’s 2016 Communication Design Graduates. It marked the beginning of a new degree which has been restructured into specialised pathways – advertising, branding, communication arts, graphic design and UX/UI.

THE PROJECT

This was a collaborative student driven project to brand the AUT Communication Design Graduate Exhibition for 2016.

THE OUTCOME

With a core team of three designers, the branding for the exhibition was developed initially around the idea of the new degree and the specialised pathways. This eventually turned into a expression of our journey through the course and the coming of our launch into our design careers, hence the name ’Set in Motion.’
The final brand reflects a student-like approach that is a bit weird, yet curious and playful. Using a bold red and white palette, the brand reflects a confident and dynamic personality. The in-motion birds, and the pinball that rolled within the customised version of the Brandon Grotesque typeface, added an overall playful and curious tone to the brand, reflecting the curiosity and wonder of young designers.
