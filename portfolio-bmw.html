BMW

Brand Case Study

ABOUT

The Ultimate Driving Machine. It’s a slogan that encapsulates BMW’s brand story, essence and promise all into one bold, powerful and memorable statement.
BMW is a German automobile, motorcycle and engine manufacturing company that was founded in 1916. Highly regarded for their close to perfection engineering, BMW is now iconic for its range of luxury, sports and family automobiles and motorcycles. The key to their success comes from a strong dedication and constant pursuit of quality and excellence.

THE PROJECT

The project was to research and compile a comprehensive case study on the iconic brand, BMW, and present it in a publication reflective of the brand’s core essence. This prompted a deeper and more thorough understanding of the brand today that can be traced back to its roots and history.

THE OUTCOME

BMW is a luxury brand that strives for quality, perfection and excellence and this has been reflected in the publication.
I wanted the case study to reflect their long, rich history and constant pursuit of quality, perfection and excellence, and how that translates into the brand it is today. The design reflects BMW’s visual identity with crisp full bleed images, clean typography and a consistent balanced layout.
