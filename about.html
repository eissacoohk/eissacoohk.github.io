A DESIGNER.

Hi! I’m Cassie Khoo, a designer based in Auckland. I’m currently studying a Master of Design at AUT, working on a project that explores how we might co-design a public health brand and online platform for and with young people.

Design has the potential to do good. To improve and better people’s lives. As a designer I strive to take on projects that share those same values, using my passion for design and desire to problem solve through research to design effective solutions.
