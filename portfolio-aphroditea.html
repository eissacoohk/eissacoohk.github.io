ABOUT

Aphroditea Tea Ltd, is a online detox tea business, supplying healthy teas to New Zealand and Australia.
Check them out here! www.aphroditea.co.nz

THE PROJECT

The project was to develop a visual identity for this upcoming detox tea business to help launch it into the market. This included the development of packaging, brochures, informative and instructional pieces, and other collateral. A website design was also developed.
